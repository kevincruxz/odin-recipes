Eggs with Ham

Tipical mexican breakfast, easy and quickly to do.

With this dish you do not have to put so much effort or to be very good cooking, you only have to do 3 easy steps
First put oil on one pan, then spread little pieces of ham, and after 2-3 minutes add eggs, that's it.

Ingredients.

  • Oil.
  • Ham.
  • Eggs.

Steps.

  1. Add some oil to the pan.
  2. Spread little pieces of ham all over the pan.
  3. Wait until the ham turns it's color to red.
  4. Add some eggs, (the quantity depends on the number of persons who are going to eat).
  5. Move the mix, in order the eggs do not stick in the pan.
  6. Eat.
