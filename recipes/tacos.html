Tacos

One of the worldwide known dishes, the most popular mexican food, tacos, tortilla with any other food inside it, so easy, or not?.

Oh tacos, a tortilla with any other dish inside it, prepare the taco is the easy and fast step, but prepare what is going to be inside the tortilla is the not so easy step.

  • Tortilla.
  • Whatever you want. (Bisteck, pastor, etc.)

Steps.

  1. First, prepare the inside part of the taco, come on i have told you, anything.
  2. Heat up the tortillas, 5 or 6 minutes before the inside food is done.
  3. Spread the food inside the tortilla.
